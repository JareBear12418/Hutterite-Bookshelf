1. Rejoice for the steps of a righteous man,
They are ordered of God, they are ordered of God.
Rejoice for the steps of a righteous man,
They are ordered of God.

2. In the time of trouble God will uphold you,
God will deliver you and God will sustain you.
In the time of trouble, God will lift you up,
So rejoice your steps are ordered of God.

3. Rejoice for the love of the Holy Ghost,
Is shed abroad in you now; is shed abroad in you now.
Rejoice for the love of the Holy Ghost,
Is shed abroad in you now.

4. His love is never ceasing; always believing,
Esteeming the other better than yourself.
His love is full of goodness, filled with tender mercy,
So rejoice for your love is of God.
