The ceremony was over;
A lad stood alone in tears.
For he had just said good-bye
To the one he had loved through the years.

CH: When God comes and gathers His jewels;
His treasures of diamonds and gold.
We'll meet her up there, up in Heaven so fair.
When God comes and gathers His jewels.

He stood all alone with his head bowed low,
As though his heart would break.
The parson came over and took his hand,
And to him these words he did say:

Each night when the pale moon was shining,
You could see this lad all alone,
With his eyes lifted up towards Heaven
